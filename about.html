Lionz Trading Ltd. is a Prime Commodity trading, Real Estate and Construction company based in Europe, the Middle East and Asia. It specializes in the trade of soft and hard commodities ranging from Grains, Sugar, Metals, Oil, Fuel and Petrochemicals. The Real Estate Portfolio has grounds from Europe through the Middle East and into Asia. European Projects being in Switzerland, France, Germany, Spain and the UK. Other Real Estate markets with our activity are UAE and Singapore. Our Construction work is involved in all our projects in these markets through our subsidiaries.


Having its operations spanning the globe, Lionz Trading Ltd. works in reference to the highest international standards in trading, trade finance, risk management, real estate investments and construction operations. Lionz Trading Ltd. enjoys a professional team with over 4 Decades of work in theseindustries, which aligns with the owners’ vision of building a world class organization that will have a leading role in the global economy for years to come.
